# Ministry Roadmap

This document captures the major build stages for the online campus.

## Stage 1: Clarify

- Define the purpose of the online campus
- Decide what success means
- Name the major responsibilities currently held by the online campus pastor

## Stage 2: Stabilize

- Document weekly operations
- Standardize checklists and recurring workflows
- Reduce single-person dependency

## Stage 3: Delegate

- Separate responsibilities into clear roles
- Create serve team-friendly job descriptions
- Build onboarding and training materials

## Stage 4: Measure

- Establish a weekly scorecard
- Track follow-up pipelines and response times
- Surface missed handoffs and recurring bottlenecks

## Stage 5: Scale

- Expand serve team ownership
- Improve systems integration and automation
- Review ministry effectiveness by role, not just by event
