# Serve Team Development Path

This document outlines a simple pathway for recruiting and developing serve team members for the online campus.

## 1. Identify the role

- Choose a specific role instead of recruiting for "help"
- Define clear responsibilities, time expectations, and boundaries

## 2. Create the role description

- Purpose
- Weekly responsibilities
- Skills required
- Traits desired
- Training required
- Owner or supervisor

Use [Role Description Template](../../templates/role-description-template.md).

## 3. Recruit intentionally

- Invite people based on fit
- Explain why the role matters pastorally, not just operationally
- Clarify schedule expectations up front

## 4. Train for success

- Give written documentation
- Provide shadowing opportunities
- Practice likely scenarios
- Clarify what to escalate

## 5. Release with support

- Start with a defined assignment
- Debrief after early serving experiences
- Improve documentation based on real friction points

## 6. Develop leaders

- Identify reliable serve team members who can coach others
- Move mature serve team members toward ownership of repeatable processes
