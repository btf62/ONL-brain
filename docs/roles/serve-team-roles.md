# Serve Team Roles

These are likely serve team roles that can eventually be staffed, trained, and scheduled with confidence.

## Chat host

Purpose:
Create a welcoming and responsive chat environment during services.

Typical responsibilities:

- Welcome people in chat
- Prompt engagement
- Respond to basic questions
- Surface prayer and care needs
- Encourage next steps

## Online moderator

Purpose:
Maintain a healthy, safe, and focused chat environment.

Typical responsibilities:

- Monitor chat behavior
- Enforce moderation standards
- Flag sensitive or escalating situations
- Support hosts behind the scenes

## Prayer responder

Purpose:
Offer timely, compassionate initial response to prayer needs within defined boundaries.

Typical responsibilities:

- Acknowledge requests
- Respond appropriately
- Escalate urgent concerns
- Help ensure follow-up is captured

## Service support

Purpose:
Support operational readiness and repeatable weekend tasks.

Typical responsibilities:

- Verify links and forms
- Help prepare host notes
- Confirm checklist completion
- Support post-service reporting

## Technical support

Purpose:
Support stream setup, monitoring, and issue escalation.

Typical responsibilities:

- Help verify platform readiness
- Monitor stream quality
- Document incidents
- Assist with contingency steps

## Notes

Each of these should eventually get its own role description, training guide, and success checklist.
