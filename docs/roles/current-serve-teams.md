# Current Serve Teams

This document captures the current formal serve team structure for the online campus.

It should be updated when teams are added, retired, or materially restructured.

## Rock RMS structure

Rock currently represents the online campus serve teams this way:

- `Online Campus`: Rock group `57902`, group type `Serve Team Section`
- `Administration`: Rock group `62713`, parent `Online Campus`, group type `Serve Team`
- `Chat Hosts`: Rock group `57903`, parent `Online Campus`, group type `Serve Team`
- `Production`: Rock group `59363`, parent `Online Campus`, group type `Serve Team`
- `Starting Point Leaders`: Rock group `61877`, parent `Online Campus`, group type `Serve Team`

Rock is the best source for roster lookup, but the active Rock roster may include staff, leaders, legacy members, or people who are not part of the current schedulable core.

Known cleanup note:

- the `Starting Point Leaders` Rock group appears obsolete for current online campus operations because that workflow has not been active for years
- the active members in the `Administration` Rock group are former serve team members and should likely be archived in Rock
- update Rock before treating those rosters as current staffing reality

## Active serve teams

### Chat Host Serve Team

Current working size:

- about seven serve team members

Rock roster note:

- Rock group: `57903`
- Rock name: `Chat Hosts`
- active, non-archived Rock members at last lookup: `9`
- Rock currently lists the online campus pastor as `Leader(Attendance)`

Purpose:

- create a welcoming and responsive chat environment during online services
- help people feel seen and invited into appropriate next steps
- surface prayer, care, and follow-up needs

Current leadership state:

- this team used to have a chat host lead
- the chat host lead handled scheduling, onboarding, training, and team oversight
- that lead role is currently a known leadership function even if it needs to be restaffed or clarified

Likely lead responsibilities:

- schedule chat hosts
- onboard new chat hosts
- train chat hosts in hospitality, escalation, and platform use
- keep the online campus pastor aware of coverage gaps or team health concerns
