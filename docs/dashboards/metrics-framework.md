# Metrics Framework

This document translates ministry activity into trackable categories for dashboards or scorecards.

## Dashboard 1: Weekly ministry scorecard

Track:

- Weekend attendance and engagement
- Chat participation
- Prayer requests
- Next-step submissions
- Follow-up completion
- Notable stories

## Dashboard 2: Pipeline health

Track:

- Open items by pipeline
- Items without owners
- Average response time
- Overdue follow-up
- Completion rate by next-step type

## Dashboard 3: Serve team and role health

Track:

- Scheduled coverage by role
- Unfilled positions
- Training completion
- Serve team retention

## Dashboard 4: Operational health

Track:

- Checklist completion
- Script readiness
- Service incidents
- Recovery actions completed

## Design principle

The first dashboards should be simple enough to update manually. Automation can come later after the metrics prove useful.
