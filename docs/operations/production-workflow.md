# Production Workflow

This document describes the repeatable flow for preparing and delivering the online service experience.

## 1. Plan the weekend

- Identify the service focus, message, calls to action, and special elements
- Determine what the online audience specifically needs to hear or do
- Decide whether any online-only content is required

## 2. Prepare assets

- Scripts
- Video clips
- Graphics and lower thirds
- Links and QR destinations
- Chat prompts and host notes

## 3. Staff the service

- Assign host and moderator coverage
- Confirm technical operators if needed
- Clarify escalation paths for prayer, care, and disruptive chat behavior

## 4. Rehearse and verify

- Confirm timing and service order
- Test stream setup
- Check audio and video quality
- Validate links, forms, and response paths

## 5. Execute

- Start strong with clear host presence
- Maintain active hospitality in chat
- Escalate prayer and care needs quickly
- Document issues in real time

## 6. Follow through

- Capture responses in Rock RMS
- Assign or complete follow-up
- Report numbers, stories, and incidents
- Improve the process before the next weekend
