## 360 Reviews / Deep & Wide

Canonical systems:

- HR email instructions from Sharon Hanchett
- Google Forms linked from HR
- final review conversation with supervisor(s)

Annual shape from 2025 and 2026 email evidence:

1. Self-assessment begins the process.
2. Deep & Wide review assignments follow.
3. Assigned reviews include:
   - `DEEP` reviews for people Brad interacts with frequently or supervises
   - `WIDE` reviews for people Brad works with occasionally
   - self-assessment
   - `DEEP SELF` assessment
4. Responses are confidential; final packets exclude reviewer names.
5. Review conversation with supervisor(s) follows after assignments are complete.
6. Goal-setting and role/job-description review are part of the supervisor conversation.

## Suggested cadence

Annual:

- block time for Annual Staff Requirements as soon as the course opens
- block multiple writing sessions for self-assessment and Deep & Wide reviews
- archive non-confidential instructions and prompts
- privately preserve past review inputs for future reference

Weekly during review season:

- check whether new review assignments have arrived
- choose a small closed list of reviews or self-assessment sections to complete
- avoid treating all reviews as one giant task
