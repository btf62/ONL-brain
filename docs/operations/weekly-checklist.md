# Weekly Checklist

Use this checklist to run the recurring weekly work of the online campus.

## Early week

- Confirm upcoming service plan, message, and any online-specific moments
- Review scripts, announcements, and required video assets
- Confirm serve team coverage for hosts, moderators, and technical roles
- Review outstanding follow-up items from the previous weekend
- Check for any special campaigns, baptism moments, or calls to action

## Midweek

- Finalize scripts for online-only segments and announcements
- Verify media assets, lower thirds, links, and forms
- Confirm Rock RMS pathways for expected responses
- Prepare host notes, talking points, and escalation instructions
- Review service run-of-show and technical readiness

## Weekend pre-service

- Confirm stream destination, timing, and platform readiness
- Verify audio, video, graphics, and internet stability
- Make sure hosts know who is serving, when to arrive, and how to respond
- Confirm prayer, baptism, membership, and serving links are active
- Review contingency plan for technical failure or emergency handoff

## During service

- Monitor stream health
- Support hosts and moderators in real time
- Capture notable pastoral moments and follow-up needs
- Document technical issues for post-service review
- Record any immediate next-step responses needing same-day action

## Post-service

- Close out outstanding chat conversations
- Enter or verify follow-up items in Rock RMS
- Route prayer, care, baptism, membership, and serving responses appropriately
- Debrief issues, wins, and notable stories
- Gather core analytics and update the weekly scorecard

## Weekly review

- What worked well?
- What created avoidable stress?
- What follow-up is still open?
- Which responsibilities should be documented better?
- Which tasks could move to a serve team member or another staff role?
