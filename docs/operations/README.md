# Operations Overview

Operations is the week-by-week production and execution engine of the online campus.

This includes everything required to prepare, deliver, and review the online ministry experience each week.

## Scope

- Service planning and preparation
- Studio or streaming readiness
- Script writing and content preparation
- Chat host scheduling and support
- Analytics gathering and reporting
- Follow-up handoffs after the service

## Operational goals

- Every service is prepared and delivered with excellence
- Serve team members know their assignments and expectations
- Technical failures are reduced through preparation and documentation
- Important follow-up work is handed off instead of forgotten
- Repeating tasks are captured in systems, not memory
