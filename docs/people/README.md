# People Overview

People work is the pastoral side of the online campus.

This area exists to ensure the online ministry is not merely producing content, but actually caring for people and helping them take meaningful next steps.

## Scope

- Prayer requests
- Pastoral follow-up
- Baptism and membership pathways
- Text HELLO check-ins
- Serving conversations
- Group or community next steps
- Key leader care
- Community group leader coaching
- Care escalation and referral

## Outcomes

- People feel seen and responded to
- Requests do not disappear after the service
- Clear handoffs exist between hosts, pastor, and follow-up systems
- Rock RMS reflects real ministry activity
- People are invited into appropriate next steps
- Key leaders and community group leaders receive intentional touchpoints instead of only urgent contact
