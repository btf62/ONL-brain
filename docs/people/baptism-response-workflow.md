# Baptism Response Workflow

Source documents:

- `Baptism_Membership - Campus_Central Tasks.xlsx`
- `Baptism/Membership - Campus/Central Tasks`
- [Central/Campus Playbook: Baptism](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1HxJkubSlo0_yha-rI-B7ozaDcgszTF2fxd7TfmqjBT4)

## Purpose

This document captures the working baptism inquiry and application response flow for the online campus.

The goal is to make sure baptism interest receives timely pastoral response, enters the correct Rock pipeline, and moves toward interview, video story, scheduling, baptism day, and follow-up documentation.

## Response target

- First response target: within 24 to 48 hours.
- The response should affirm the person's interest, clarify the next step, and make sure the person knows they are not waiting in a void.

## Core ownership pattern

The source spreadsheet divides the baptism process between Campus and Central.

Campus responsibilities generally include:

- initial contact and pastoral follow-up
- baptism interview
- video shoot scheduling
- baptism date scheduling
- baptism practice/preparation
- baptism day
- sharing the video and picture link with the baptizee/baptizer, according to the newer Google Sheet

Central responsibilities generally include:

- notification to campus
- adding or maintaining the person in the Rock baptism pipeline
- receiving application responses
- compiling application information
- notifying campus
- sending confirmation email to the person getting baptized
- shooting/editing the baptism video story, according to the newer Google Sheet
- baptism supplies
- post-baptism documentation
- weekly update notification
- sharing video and picture link with the campus team

## Workflow

### 1. Initial contact

Owner:

- Campus

Action:

- send the applicant the pre-work link
- follow up with the applicant as needed
- make sure the person understands the next step and who will contact them

Online campus note:

- if the inquiry comes through `iwant.info`, email, or another online source, confirm whether Central has already responded before duplicating contact
- if no response is visible, the online campus pastor should make a timely pastoral acknowledgment while the handoff is clarified

### 2. Notify campus and update Rock

Owner:

- Central

Action:

- notify the Campus Pastor of the application
- add the applicant to the Rock baptism pipeline
- follow up as needed

Open question:

- source notes ask whether Campus should own initial communication while Central owns the Rock pipeline

### 3. Receive and organize the baptism application

Owner:

- Central

Action:

- receive baptism application responses
- compile responses into a Google Doc
- notify the campus
- send a confirmation email to the person getting baptized

### 4. Baptism interview

Owner:

- Campus

Action:

- schedule a meeting to hear the person's story and gospel understanding
- review the Baptism Interviewer Job Description before the interview
- determine whether the person is ready to continue toward baptism

### 5. Video shoot scheduling and preparation

Owner:

- Campus

Action:

- schedule the video shoot with the person getting baptized and the video team
- use the baptism shoot scheduling link when applicable
- make a copy of the Baptism Video Shoot Template
- add the baptizee's name to the document title
- send the template to the baptizee for review before the shoot
- review the Baptism Video Interviewer Job Description before the video interview

Source link:

- `https://koalendar.com/e/baptism-shoot`

### 6. Video shoot and edit

Owner:

- Central

Action:

- shoot and edit the baptism video story

Note:

- the native Google Sheet includes this as a distinct Central task; the `.xlsx` search result did not surface it as a separate row

### 7. Schedule baptism date

Owner:

- Campus

Action:

- after the video shoot, schedule the baptism date with the campus
- add or update the person in the Rock baptism pipeline
- notify Central by email
- contact the photography team for pictures

### 8. Baptism practice and preparation

Owner:

- Campus

Action:

- notify the baptism team, baptizer, and baptizee of baptism date details
- practice baptism as needed
- read the Practice Baptism job description before practice

### 9. Baptism day

Owner:

- Campus

Action:

- support the baptizee and baptizer
- execute the baptism plan
- capture any follow-up needs or pastoral notes

### 10. Supplies

Owner:

- Central

Action:

- once the baptism date is scheduled, provide t-shirts, baptism certificate, and gift

### 11. Documentation after baptism

Owner:

- Central

Action:

- change the Rock status
- update baptism and membership tracking
- notify the office manager for the weekly update on Monday after baptism

### 12. Share video and pictures

Owner:

- Central shares video and picture link with the campus team
- Campus shares video and picture link with the baptizee/baptizer

Note:

- the exact ownership differs slightly between the `.xlsx` result and the newer native Google Sheet, so this should be verified against the current playbook owner

## Online campus watchpoints

- Do not let a baptism inquiry sit in email without acknowledgment.
- Confirm whether the person is already in the Rock baptism pipeline.
- Clarify whether Central has already sent the pre-work link or confirmation email.
- Track whether the baptism interview has been scheduled.
- Track whether the video shoot has been scheduled.
- Track whether a baptism date has been selected.
- Confirm post-baptism Rock status and tracking updates are complete.
